    Eligibility for Salary Advance

    Employees can request a salary advance if they have worked for at least 6 months.

  • Maximum Salary Advance

    The maximum advance allowed is 50% of the current month's salary.

  • Repayment Terms

    Salary advances must be repaid within 3 months through payroll deductions.

  • Approval Process

    All salary advances must be approved by the HR and Finance departments.

  • Interest and Fees

    No interest is charged on salary advances, but late repayment may result in penalties.

  • How to Apply

    To request a salary advance, submit a formal application to the HR department.
